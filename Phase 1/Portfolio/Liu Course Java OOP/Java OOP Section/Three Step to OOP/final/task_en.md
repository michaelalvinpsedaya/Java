## `final` keyword

- When `final` is used with a variable (or attribute), this variable becomes a constant and is not allowed to change (reassign)
- When `final` is used with a method, it indicates the method is at its "end," it can be inherited by subclasses but cannot be overridden
- When `final` is used with a class, it indicates the class is at its "end" and cannot have subclasses

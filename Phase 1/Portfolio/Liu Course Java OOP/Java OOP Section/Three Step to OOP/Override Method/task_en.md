## override

- **When a subclass redefines and implements a method of its superclass, it is referred to as method overriding.**
- The practical significance of method overriding is that the subclass inherits the main behavior of the superclass but can implement its own behavior. For example, all animals have the method of hunting, but different animals like fish, eagles, and chickens have different ways of hunting.
- Method overriding is observed from a **vertical** perspective of class behavior.
